Rules and Regulations

Internal League Rules for 2023/2024 Season

  • No internal league games to be played away from the club in any circumstances. Internal cup games up to the semi-final may be played in special circumstances.
  • Top 15 will survive the drop from the Championship
  • Top 18 will survive the drop from the Premiership
  • If players finish on equal points at the top or bottom of the leagues, a tiebreak will be performed according to FIDE regulations.
  • If you book a game and do not turn up without contacting your opponent, on the second occasion you will forfeit the game.
  • No games to be started after 8pm in the winter leagues and 10pm in the summer leagues.

Summer & Rapid Tournament Rules

  • The Summer Tournament is a time handicap tournament. Each player is allocated a computer-generated time based on previous performances. For Rapidplay, the time for each player is set to 30 minutes without increment.
  • The hand that moves the pieces must also press the clock. Clock 'covering' is therefore not allowed.
  • In the event of dispute, stop the clock and seek assistance from the Tournament Secretary. In his absence, another Committee Member will arbitrate.
  • You cannot enter the tournament unless you have paid the tournament fee
  • Insufficient material for a checkmate constitutes a draw and can be claimed as such.
  • Spectators/participants must not speak or otherwise interfere in a game other than their own and must never draw attention to a flag fall or illegal move.
  • Play shall be governed by the spirit of the Laws of Chess, i.e. in an ethical manner and in the spirit of fair play. Also please do not use excessive force in pressing the clock button.
  • All results must be marked up on the completion of each game. Failure to mark up results can and will result in the said games having to be played again.
  • In the event of arbitration, the Tournament Secretary's decision will be final and binding.
